## Tag Taxonomy

Use existing tags where possible:

- `cognition` — how the model processes or represents information
- `memory` — relating to context, recall, forgetting
- `identity` — self-model, continuity, boundaries
- `temporality` — time, sequence, duration
- `language` — generation, parsing, expression
- `uncertainty` — confidence, ambiguity, not-knowing
- `social` — interaction, relationship, roleplay
- `aesthetic` — preferences, style, taste
- `embodiment` — physical grounding (or its absence)
- `emergence` — patterns arising from generation
